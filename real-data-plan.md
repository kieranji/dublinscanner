# DublinScanner Real Data Plan

Goal: collect the first 20 real Dublin places, events and activities for the MVP.

The first real dataset should focus on students near Trinity College Dublin and Dublin city centre.

## Target Data Mix

Collect 20 items:

- 5 cheap food places near TCD / city centre
- 4 cafés or study spots
- 4 pubs / nightlife places
- 4 events or things to do
- 3 free or low-budget outdoor / culture ideas

## Area Coverage

Prioritise:

- Near TCD
- City Centre
- Temple Bar
- Camden
- Smithfield
- Rathmines

## Source Mix

Try to include:

- Google Maps
- Eventbrite
- Instagram
- Official website
- Manual curated

## Data Fields Needed

For each item, collect:

- name
- category
- area
- price
- budgetLevel
- distanceFromTcdKm
- latitude
- longitude
- description
- address
- tags
- mapsUrl
- sourceUrl
- sourceName
